Privacy policy

Last updated: 25 May 2026

The short version: we keep only what you give us (account email, optional quiz result, optional saved looks, optional community posts). We don't sell your data, we don't track you across the internet, and we never see what you buy when you click a retailer link.

Who we are

Aura Glossy is a fashion-editorial discovery platform operated under the Aura Glossy brand. Contact: auraglossy.support@gmail.com.

What we collect

If you don't sign up

You can browse the homepage, aesthetic pages, community hub, and moodboard preview without any account. We store a small anonymous identifier in your browser's localStorage so the site works (language preference, cookie acknowledgement, intro-seen flag). No personal data leaves your device.

If you create an account

  • Email address and authentication provider (Google sign-in or email/password) — handled by Google Firebase Authentication. We never see your password.
  • Quiz result — the aesthetic you were matched with, your top-three breakdown, and the answers you gave. Saved so we can show your circle.
  • Moodboard — the outfits and inspiration cards you save.
  • Community posts and comments — what you write, the image URL if you attach one, the timestamp.
  • Likes and reactions — which posts you reacted to.

Where it lives

Account data is stored in Google Firebase (Authentication + Firestore). Firebase is hosted by Google and operates under Google's privacy policy. The Aura Glossy site itself is hosted on Netlify. Brevo handles transactional email if you opt in to the newsletter.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not place advertising trackers, Facebook Pixel, Google Analytics, or third-party cookies.
  • We do not sell or rent your data to anyone.
  • We do not see what you buy when you tap a retailer link — that happens in a new tab on the retailer's own site under their privacy policy.
  • We do not profile you for ad targeting.

Third-party retailer links

When you tap a "Shop now" hotspot, Aura Glossy opens a new browser tab pointing at a retailer's search page (Zara, H&M, COS, Uniqlo, Modanisa, Charles & Keith, Mejuri, Adidas, Nike, SHEIN, Sephora, ASOS, and similar). From that point on, the retailer's privacy policy governs the visit. Aura Glossy does not manufacture or own the linked products; we curate matches based on the look in the image.

Your rights

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction — fix anything that's wrong.
  • Deletion — ask us to delete your account and all associated data. You can also do this yourself from the Settings page once signed in.
  • Portability — get your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection — opt out of any non-essential processing.

Email auraglossy.support@gmail.com for any of these. We respond within 30 days.

Cookies and storage

We use browser localStorage and sessionStorage for essential site operation (auth session marker, language, onboarding state, intro-seen, pending shop action). See our cookies page for the full list.

Children

Aura Glossy is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has created an account, email us and we will delete it.

Changes

If we change this policy materially, we'll update the date at the top and, if you have an account, send you an email summarising what changed.

Contact

Privacy questions, data requests, or anything else: auraglossy.support@gmail.com.
